Executive Summary – Highlights as at 30-11-2018
                                                                                                                                                  T    O
<U+F071> The Group Capital position has strenghtened in November, thanks to the planned deleverage on the credit side and the lowering Italian spread on the financial
    side. At the end of November the Total Capital Ratio recovers from the Tolerance level breach. Both the TC and CET1 Ratios are higher than the respective Preco
    2018 values.
                                                                                                                                   IT     A
<U+F071> The short term liquidity profile continue to be positive; the LCR is slightly increasing mainly because of the proceedings from non elegible asset disposals. On the
    other hand the medium/long term profile is still weak due to, as in the previous month, a decreasing commercial funding. Both the NSFR and the Gap Ratios reflect
                                                                                                                             M
    such dynamics: in November the NSFR is confirmed under the risk limit and is approaching the Tolerance; the Gap Ratio 3y is confirmed below its Tolerance level.
                                                                                                                        O
<U+F071> The Pillar 1 RWAs exhibit a higher than 3 €/bln decrease, that is around 3% of the Regulatory amount. As said, the more significant reduction concerns the credit
                                                                                                                C
    and counterparty risk. Within the Pillar 2 RWA the Market Risk Banking Book has increased.
Some more details:
                                                                                                         E
<U+F071> RWAs of the AIRB portfolio decrease substantially: on one hand the deleveraging actions have lowered the credit exposures, mainly on the corporate portfolio; on
                                                                                                  N
    the other hand positive results, mainly on the retail portfolio, come from the optimisation of the supporting factor, the LGD floor and from a more effective way of
    detection of Consap/MCC warranties.
                                                                                         I  O
<U+F071> The monthly credit strenghtening is almost flat, with the cumulated value now slightly below the target. In November is observed a brake on PD’s decreasing trend;
                                                                                  Z
    the PD level remains anyway far lower than the target, while the LGD has decreased. The deleverage, in fact, has affected mainly the «stable» positions (those
    leading the PD reduction) and the unsecured exposures (higher LGD), with a positive net effect on the RWA. The cost of credit remains under the budget target,
                                                                            A
    with an increasing difference. On the negative side, also the gap regarding the bad loans portfolio recovery rate is widening.
                                                                        T
<U+F071> Regarding the Pillar 2 Market Risk, the Trading Portfolio RWAs increase again because of the inclusion, witin the Stressed VaR time window, of some more recent
                                                                     N
    significantly negative scenarios for the Italian spread. Similarly, the Pillar 2 RWAs of the Banking Book reflect the inclusion, in the rolling 1 year average, of the
                                                             E
    november 2018 monthly average. The rebalancing from the OCI to the AC Portfolio has been carried on, in November, mainly through a reduction of the OCI
    portfolio; that, along with the lower Italian spread, explain the slight reduction in the overall (FV+AC) portfolio VaR.
Risk Limits:
                                             U      M
<U+F071> In November some indicators recover from breaching the risk limits: the Exposure Amount (performing) on the CCO Corporate, the Banking Portfolio Value (VPB)
                                     C
    and, thanks to the authorised infra-Group deposits operativity, the WIDIBA IRRBB.
